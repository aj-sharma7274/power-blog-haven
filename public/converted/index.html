Power Apps is a suite of apps, services, and connectors, as well as a data platform, that provides a rapid development environment to build custom apps for your business needs. Using Power Apps, you can quickly build custom business apps that connect to your data stored either in the underlying data platform (Microsoft Dataverse) or in various online and on-premises data sources.

Benefits of Power Apps

  • Build apps quickly with a visual designer
  • Connect to your data easily
  • Publish instantly to web and mobile
  • Customize apps to match your business needs

Getting Started

To get started with Power Apps, you'll need a Microsoft 365 subscription or a Power Apps license. Once you have access, you can start creating apps using the web-based Power Apps Studio.

Creating Your First App

Power Apps offers several ways to create apps:

  1. Start from a template
  2. Start from data (e.g., SharePoint list, Excel file)
  3. Start from scratch with a blank canvas

For beginners, starting from a template or from your data is the easiest way to get familiar with the platform.
